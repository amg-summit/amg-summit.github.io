The AMG Summit

A research workshop in the high Rocky Mountains for the advancement of algebraic multigrid methods and related methods.
Schedule History Participants Sponsors Current Topics


The policy of the Summit is that the participants leave their polished talks and reticence behind in favor of burning questions, open problems, new ideas, and readiness to interact.
